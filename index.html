About Me

A Passion for Visual Storytelling

Hello! I'm Alex Morgan, a professional freelance photographer based in New York City. With over 8 years of experience behind the lens, I've developed a distinctive style that blends technical precision with artistic vision.

My journey began with landscape photography in the mountains of Colorado, and has since expanded to include portraits, weddings, commercial work, and editorial photography for major publications.

Every photograph I take is a collaboration — between light and shadow, subject and environment, emotion and composition. I believe the best images are those that make you feel something.
